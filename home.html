WELCOME TO KALEO ROMAN CATHOLIC JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL

This is the official page of Kaleo Roman catholic junior high school, it is a government school located in the upper west region, specifically, in the Nadowli-Kaleo District. It was started in the year 1985, with a total number of fifty-three students which comprises thirty-two(32) males and twenty-one(21) females. On this page, you can find information about assignments, exams, timetables, subjects, syllabuses and teachers of this school.

The number of students keeps increasing as time goes on,since the performance of students in the school is very good.
