Subjects

English:
English classes focus on developing language skills in reading, writing, speaking, and listening. Students study literature, grammar, vocabulary, and composition. It helps improve communication abilities and understanding of literary works.

Mathematics:
Mathematics involves studying numbers, shapes, patterns, and relationships. It helps students develop problem-solving skills and understand concepts like algebra, geometry, and statistics.

Integrated science:
Integrated Science integrates different scientific disciplines such as biology, chemistry, physics, and earth science. Students learn about scientific principles, conduct experiments, and explore how science impacts daily life and the natural world.

Social studies:
Social Studies explores human society and its interactions. It covers topics such as history, geography, civics, economics, and culture. Students learn about different cultures, historical events, governments, and global issues.

Career Technology:
Introduces students to practical skills and knowledge related to various careers and industries. It includes subjects like computer science, business, agriculture, healthcare and trades, preparing students for future professions.

Dagaare:
It is a Ghanaian language, mostly spoken by the Dagaabas.It is taught in school so as to enable students acquire knowledge about their culture and believes.
